House of Music - Vienna

An unique musical experience in the heart of Vienna

About the museum

The House of Music is a unique interactive museum in Vienna, dedicated to the fascinating world of music and sounds. Visitors can explore the history of famous composers and experiment with musical instruments.

Interactive experiences

Visitors can virtually conduct the Vienna Philharmonic Orchestra, create digital music, and interact with multimedia exhibits. It is an educational and entertaining experience for all ages.
